Kilalanin si Master

GUIDED BY OUR PASSION FOR BUSINESS, OUR MISSION IS TO EMPOWER FILIPINO SME OWNERS TO DIVE HEADFIRST INTO THE WORLD OF E-COMMERCE WHILE BRINGING FRESH, PROMISING BRANDS TO OUR CUSTOMERS.

Our History

MasterMovers was born out of the desire to improve the Filipinos’ shopping experience.

We envision a world where Filipino shoppers have access to products anytime, anywhere, and Filipino Micro, Small and medium (MSMEs) owners are empowered to dominate the business world.

Displeased with the long waiting time when it comes to ordering products online, MasterMovers seeks to offer a better shopping experience for Filipinos by connecting with several business owners and having a same-day delivery system.

At MasterMovers, we seek to give opportunities to Filipinos- may they be consumers, business owners, or riders. Launched in July 2022, MasterMovers works with a team of hardworking, innovative problem-solvers who are determined to create an app that connects consumers with Filipino businesses.

MISSION

At MasterMovers, we seek to bring the best of both worlds: fresh, promising brands and eager, street-smart consumers.

OUR goal is to support our clients' businesses as an extension by handling all of their courier needs and offering them industry-leading transportation management solutions that correspond to our key principles of dependability, accuracy, integrity, and service. We always go above for our customers.

Making planned deliveries and time-sensitive products by providing ka-MasterShops (MSMEs) and ka-Master Followers (our dear customers) with same-day delivery options are our specialties. For daily pick-ups and deliveries from your place of Home, we offer a practical alternative.

All of our ka-MASTER Drivers have received thorough training and evaluation on the need for confidentiality, and we have also required each of them to sign a non-disclosure agreement. You may relax knowing that your business, your parcels as well as your future is in good and capable hands.

Because of the following reasons, you can rest easy knowing that your company is in good hands while using MasterMovers’ same-day delivery service:

  • We promise that your supply chain's integrity.
  • We prioritize deliveries that arrive within 24 hours.
  • We provide same-day delivery, sometimes even within an hour for urgent important items.
  • We are a highly specialized, client-driven company with a primary goal of providing customers with "just-in-time" delivery of their packages.
  • Within the immediate area, we pick up and transport important business documents, packages, and parts.
  • We send packages that the sender is unable to dependably dispatch by other means due to time or other restrictions.
